## Ball types

- Multiplier
Multiplies an impact (explosion, arrow, bouncy) on break
- Explosion
Explodes
- Normal
just kinda breaks
- Bouncy
crashes into other balls and breaks at the end of the turn
- Gravity
pulls balls close to it
